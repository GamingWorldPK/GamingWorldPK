Common Questions
How do referral links work? +
Each game listing has a unique referral link embedded with a NexusPlay tracking code. When someone signs up through your link, you earn the reward listed on that game's card — coins, in-game currency, or cash depending on the game's program.
Is NexusPlay free to use? +
Yes, 100% free. You can browse all games, copy referral links, and earn rewards without ever paying a cent or creating an account.
How do I submit a game for listing? +
Use the contact form on this page and select "Submit Game." Include the game's name, official URL, and any referral program details. Our team will review and respond within 5 business days.
Can I become a NexusPlay partner? +
Absolutely. We work with game studios, content creators, and gaming communities. Select "Partnership" in the contact form or email partners@nexusplay.gg directly.
How are games vetted and ranked? +
Our team manually reviews every game for quality, fairness, and community reputation before listing. Rankings are determined by community reviews and referral activity — never by paid placements.
