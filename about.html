Our Story

NexusPlay was born from a simple idea: make it easier to discover great games and get rewarded for spreading the word.

Our Mission

We Connect Players to Opportunities

NexusPlay started in 2023 when a small group of passionate gamers noticed a gap: amazing games with great referral programs, but no single place to discover them all.

We built the platform we always wanted — a clean, fast, trustworthy hub where you can find top-rated games, grab your unique referral link, and start earning real rewards just by sharing what you love.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🔍

Transparency First

Every referral link is labeled. Every reward is disclosed. We believe gamers deserve to know exactly what they're clicking and what they'll earn — no hidden strings attached.

🛡️

Quality Over Quantity

We manually vet every game in our catalog. If a title doesn't meet our standards for fun, fairness, and community reputation, it doesn't make the cut — period.

👥

Community Driven

Our rankings and featured lists are shaped by real player reviews and activity. The community decides what rises to the top — not advertising budgets.

⚡

Always Improving

We push updates weekly — new games, better tools, fresh data. Our team is obsessed with making NexusPlay faster, smarter, and more rewarding with every release.

How We Got Here

Our Journey

From a weekend project to a platform serving tens of thousands of gamers worldwide — here's how NexusPlay grew.

JANUARY 2023
The Idea

Alex and Jordan sketch the first wireframes over Discord. The concept: one link hub for all gaming referrals.

APRIL 2023
Beta Launch

NexusPlay goes live with 12 games and 200 early testers. The community response is overwhelming.

SEPTEMBER 2023
50 Games Milestone

Priya joins to lead partnerships. The catalog grows to 50 vetted titles. 5,000 active users.

2024
Rapid Expansion

100+ games listed. Referral coins system launches. Community crosses 30K active players.
